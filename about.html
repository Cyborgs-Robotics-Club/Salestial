Welcome to Salestial

Salestial is a dynamic and exciting techfest competition that brings together the brightest minds in the tech community. Join us for a series of innovative and challenging events!

Event 1: Code Battle

A fast-paced coding competition where participants will compete to solve challenging problems in a limited time.

Event 2: Robotics Challenge

Design and build a robot that can complete a series of tasks. Test your skills in engineering and problem-solving.

Event 3: Hackathon

Form teams to create innovative tech solutions over 24 hours. Collaborate and bring your ideas to life!

About Salestial

Salestial is more than just a techfest — it’s a community where innovation, teamwork, and creativity thrive. We believe in pushing boundaries and making an impact in the tech world. Join us and be part of this exciting journey!
